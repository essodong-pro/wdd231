Site Name

Selected Name: Discover Togo

Reasoning: Building upon my previous work, this name directly clarifies the portal's goal. It serves as an open, engaging invitation for tourists, historians, and researchers to explore the rich culture, geography, and real-time data of Togo.

Optional Domain Availability: discover-togo.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Discover Togo website is to act as a comprehensive digital gateway to the country. It scales up my initial project by providing interactive logistical data (currency, transport, regional profiles), a live dynamic weather tracking hub for major cities like Lomé, and curated cultural itineraries. The goal is to drive regional visibility and serve both travel planners and cultural learners.

Target Audience Scenarios

To guide content generation and interface priorities, the architecture addresses these core audience requirements:

  • Scenario 1: "I am planning a week-long vacation to West Africa and landing in Lomé. Where can I find immediate data on local transportation, currency conversion, and current seasonal weather constraints?"
  • Scenario 2: "I am a student looking for quick facts on the primary languages, population density, and major cultural landmarks of Togo for a research paper. Is there a centralized, fast-loading dashboard?"

Typography

The typography choices optimize reading ergonomics across mixed viewing resolutions:

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (Sans-Serif)

    Applied to H1, H2, and H3 structural landmarks. Chosen for its clean architectural weight, creating strong structural visual anchors.

  • Body Font: Open Sans (Sans-Serif)

    Applied to standard layout paragraphs, tabular lists, data fields, and metadata. Offers highly optimized readability on small mobile screens.
